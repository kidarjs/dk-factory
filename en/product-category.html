Product Overview ​

Gravity Casting Products ​

Our gravity casting products include Turbines and Motor Brackets, crafted using advanced casting technology to ensure high precision and strength. These products are widely used in automotive, wind energy, and power industries, offering exceptional wear resistance and corrosion resistance to withstand demanding environments.

Turbine: Manufactured from high-quality aluminum alloys or other alloys, using precise gravity casting techniques, this product excels in high-temperature and high-pressure industrial environments.

Motor Bracket: High-strength casting designed to effectively support motors, providing minimal wear and excellent mechanical performance during prolonged operation.

Advantages

Precision casting to meet dimensional and quality standards.

Excellent corrosion resistance and high-temperature performance, prolonging service life.

Customizable designs based on client requirements to meet various engineering demands.

Application Scenarios

Turbines: Wind power, power equipment, aviation industries.

Motor Brackets: Electric motors, household appliances, machinery.

Die Casting Products Our die casting products include Baking Trays, Aluminum Clamps, and Window Frames, produced using precise die casting technology to ensure efficient production and superior surface quality. These products find application in kitchenware, machinery, and construction industries.

Baking Tray: Made of food-grade aluminum alloy with excellent heat conductivity and corrosion resistance, ensuring even heating of food.

Aluminum Clamps: High strength and durability, suitable for clamping in various machinery.

Window Frame: Strong and corrosion-resistant, ensuring structural stability for buildings.

Advantages ​

Superior surface finish for a sleek, high-quality appearance.

High-precision molds to ensure consistent part dimensions.

Rapid production cycle, reducing costs and improving efficiency.

Application Scenarios ​

Baking Trays: Household kitchens, commercial baking.

Aluminum Clamps: Machinery, industrial production lines.

Window Frames: Residential buildings, commercial buildings, factories.

Radiator Frame Our Radiator Frames are designed for high-efficiency cooling systems, suitable for various industrial equipment and automobiles. Made of aluminum alloy and processed with precise techniques, they provide excellent heat dissipation and durability.

Advantages

Excellent thermal conductivity to ensure stable equipment operation.

Lightweight design to reduce product weight.

Customizable designs to meet specific size and performance requirements.

Application Scenarios

Industrial equipment, power devices, automobile cooling systems.

Conclusion Our custom metal products, including Gravity Casting, Die Casting, and Radiator Frames, are manufactured with advanced production processes and high-quality materials. Widely applied in various industries, we are committed to providing high-performance customized products that improve the efficiency and reliability of your equipment.
